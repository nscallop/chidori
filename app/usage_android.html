Take a picture of liquor

You can see a list of logs. You can add a log by tapping the button on the bottom of the screen.

You can add a photo by taking it or picking up it from library.

Input the information of liquor

You can record data of photo, date, rate, sake and shop.

You can record a location of a shop. You can update the position of the pin by long pressing on the map.

You can search Izakaya with Foursquare by tapping the button beside shop.

Menu

You can sort the list of logs from the right menu.

You can search logs from the right menu too.

You can switch a view to the list of shops etc. from the left menu.
